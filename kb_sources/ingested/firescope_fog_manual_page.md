## ​2022​ Field Operations Guide (FOG) Information

Providing crucial information to first responders since 1983, FIRESCOPE is proud to present the interactive 2022 Field Operations Guide ICS 420-1. 
Easily access valuable information such as Wildland Fire Managem​ent Guiding Principles, Tactical Engagement, Levels of Engagement, Powerline Safety and a Structure Assessment Checklist.
